ABOUT BST INSTITUTE



Welcome to BST - Brain ShaperTechnologies, where education meets excellence. Established in 2013, BST Institute has been shaping the minds of aspiring students for a decade. We take pride in offering a wide range of programs, including BCA, MCA, and BTech, alongside a diverse array of additional courses. At BST, our experienced and highly skilled faculty are committed to providing top-notch education at affordable prices. We believe in the potential of every student, and that's why we offer a unique guarantee if a student faces difficulties and doesn't succeed in their exams, we provide free classes until they pass. With our unwavering dedication to quality education, the chances of student failure are exceptionally low. Join us at BST Institute, where we believe in nurturing talent, unlocking potential, and building a brighter future for all our students.

OUR COURSES



At BST Institute, we offer a diverse range of educational programs to cater to the aspirations of our students. Our primary focus is on BCA, MCA, and BTech programs affiliated with renowned universities such as IGNOU, MDU, SMU, and IP. In addition to our core offerings, we take pride in providing a multitude of supplementary courses that enhance students' skills and knowledge. These additional courses can be explored in the navigation menu on the left. At BST Institute, we believe in offering a comprehensive education that empowers our students for success.
